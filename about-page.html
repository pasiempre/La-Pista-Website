About
LaPista

We are redefining the pickup culture in Austin. No memberships, no commitments—just pure football for the love of the game.

Community First

"Football is the one thing that brings us all together."

Our Story

Born from the
Sidelines

LaPista started in 2025 by two brothers who love the beautiful game but struggled to find quality, consistent pickup games in Austin.

Tired of cancelled games, uneven teams, and expensive league fees, we decided to build a platform that strips away the bureaucracy and focuses on what matters: playing.

What began as a single WhatsApp group has grown into a city-wide network of players, from beginners to former pros, united by the pitch.

Our Mission

Building Austin's Community

Inclusivity

We believe football is for everyone. All backgrounds, all skill levels, all genders. No ego allowed on our fields.

Access

We unlock underutilized urban spaces and turn them into vibrant pitches. "La Pista" means "The Court" or "The Track" – anywhere can be a game.

Quality

Bibs, balls, organized teams, and dedicated hosts. We handle the logistics so you can focus on the game.

The Experience

What to Expect

Every LaPista game follows a standard to ensure safety and fun. Here is how we do things differently.

Find a Game

  • Dedicated Field Host

    A staff member is always present to organize teams, keep time, and ensure fair play.

  • Equipment Provided

    Freshly washed bibs and match-quality balls provided at every single session.

  • Post-Game Socials

    We often grab tacos or drinks after evening games. It's about the people, not just the points.
